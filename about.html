About Doctor Auto

Meet the experienced team behind Felton's premier import car service center

Our Story

For over 25 years, Doctor Auto has been the trusted choice for European and Asian import vehicle owners in Felton and the surrounding Santa Cruz Mountains. Our passion for precision engineering and commitment to excellence drives everything we do.

What started as a small garage has grown into the region's most respected import car service center, but we've never lost sight of our core values: honest service, expert craftsmanship, and treating every customer like family.
